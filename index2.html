LETTER for U

ABOUT

MISSION

With so many things to do, we might lose ourselves in the process if we fail to leave time for ourselves. Allot time for yourselves and pause for a moment to reflect. Letter for U is a love letter writing service that allows users to send themselves a letter. Write yourselves a letter and send love to yourselves!

VISION

Letter for U aspires to be a haven for individuals who want to rest by giving them opportunity to express themselves and have a time of their own. Through this, Letter for U envisions a community composed of individuals capable of loving and caring for themselves. By being able to love yourself, you only not learn to love others, but you also teach them the importance of self-love.

CONTACTS

SELF

Feeling lost and tired? Slowly take a deep breath and exhale. Pause for a while and allow yourself to rest. Everyone deserves to get their rest once in a while in order to be able to move forward again. Each day we get 24 hours and most of it goes to school, work, or sleep. However, do we still try to make time for ourselves despite our busy routines? Have no one to talk to? Try talking first to yourself. Reflect. Own and preserve your story, your life.

FAMILY

Still feeling lost and tired? Nothing beats a place which you could call home. Home is where you feel comfortable and secure with the company of your family. Family may be commonly regarded as a group of blood related people however it is not solely defined based on blood ties. Family can also refer to a group of people who support and love you and they include your friends.

FRIENDS

Still feeling lost and tired? Try and talk it out with your friends. When you feel like the world is giving up on you, although it may be hard, try to not hesitate and express yourself. Through thick and thin, friends will be there to support you and allow you to stand on your own.
